Project Log what happened at the bench

The record. The misses are the map — keep them all.
DAY 01
Set up the bench. Blinked the onboard LED, then an external one on the Pico. First code that made a real thing happen.
DAY 02
Got the motor running without taking a hand off. Started asking the AI for ramp functions.
DAY 03
Stepper moving. Started to understand time — pulse width, gap between pulses, all new. Watched one pulse become one step.
How to use your AI: bring a prediction, not a plea. Say what you think the code will do, then ask it to poke holes. Ask for a hint, not the answer. When you just want it to solve it for you — that's the signal to slow down.
