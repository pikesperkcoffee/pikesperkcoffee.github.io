The Open Source
Firefighter

A treatise on a new interface firefighting technology that enables the incident commander with a strategic resource at the line of contact.

Andrew Morton · Palmer Lake, Colorado
Former: City of Delta Junction VFD Assistant Chief · Donnelly Training Area (DMPBAX) Range Manager · U.S. Air Force Tech Control
This work is dedicated to Emily Barker, Nick Hutcherson, and Sydney Watson of the Rifle Helitack crew, killed in a burnover during initial attack on the Knowles Fire, western Colorado, June 27, 2026 — and to the two crewmates who survived it, and to all wildland firefighters who stand between fire and the rest of us.
